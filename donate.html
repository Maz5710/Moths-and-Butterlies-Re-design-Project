Forest Ringlet Connection Project

The endemic forest ringlet (Dodonidia helmsii), also know as Helms’ butterfly, is the only butterfly in its genus and it is only found in New Zealand.

According to the scientific community, its future is seriously at risk. The aim of this project is to prevent extinction.
